# Hero Background Video

Place your piano or guitar background video here as `hero-background.mp4`

## Requirements:
- Format: MP4
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD) recommended
- Duration: 30-60 seconds (will loop)
- Content: Someone playing piano or guitar
- Audio: Optional (will be muted)
- File size: Keep under 10MB for web performance

## Usage:
The video will play automatically in the background of the hero section with:
- Muted audio
- Loop enabled
- Low opacity (0.3)
- Cover fit to fill the screen
